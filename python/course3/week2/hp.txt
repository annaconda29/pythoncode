The central character in the series is Harry Potter, a boy who lives in the fictional town of Little Whinging, Surrey.
With his aunt, uncle, and cousin – the Dursleys – and discovers at the age of eleven that he is a wizard.
Though he lives in the ordinary world of non-magical people known as Muggles.
The wizarding world exists parallel to the Muggle world, albeit hidden and in secrecy.
His magical ability is inborn, and children with such abilities are invited to attend exclusive magic schools.
That teach the necessary skills to succeed in the wizarding world.
Harry becomes a student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
